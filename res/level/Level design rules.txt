The format is as follows:
player:
pl x y


passable wall: (platforms)
pw pt x y
wall:
wa [material] x y
backgrond:
bg [material] x y

material codes:
st: stone
dr: dirt
wd: wood
ey: eye

Entities:
en [entity code] x y [special attributes ... ]

Entity codes:
sp: spike
    sp x y [rotation]

Fluids:
en [fluid code] x y w h 

fluid codes:
wt: water
